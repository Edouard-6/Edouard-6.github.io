## Exposition
> “In a world where the portrayal of sexuality in film has long been a powerful force, a quiet revolution is underway. As filmmakers redefine the boundaries of sexualized content, questions arise about the implications of these portrayals on both artistic expression and commercial success. With streaming services offering greater freedom of content, the rules of the game are changing. How will the evolving landscape of sexualization in cinema impact audience perception, and which films will lead the charge in redefining how we view sexuality in film?”
